About the Project

The Aircraft Performance Calculator is designed to assist pilots in quickly calculating performance data, weight & balance, and wind components.

This tool features:

  • Real-time calculations for Density Altitude and Pressure Altitude.
  • Wind component analysis (Headwind/Crosswind).
  • Interactive Weight & Balance table.
  • Aircraft Profile Management with local storage persistence.

Q&A

How is performance calculated?

Performance figures are derived from standard aircraft tables using bilinear interpolation for accurate results based on pressure altitude and temperature.

Is my data saved?

Yes, aircraft profiles are saved locally in your browser's storage.

Can I use this for flight planning?

This tool is for educational and training purposes for the moment. Always refer to the official Pilot's Operating Handbook (POH) of your respective aircraft for operational data.
